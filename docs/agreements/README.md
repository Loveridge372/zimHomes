# ZimHomes Agreement Templates

This folder contains draft legal templates for ZimHomes:

- `residential-lease-agreement.md`: rental agreement between landlord and tenant.
- `property-management-agreement.md`: management agreement between property owner and ZimHomes.

These templates are starting drafts for Zimbabwe use. They should be reviewed and adapted by a qualified Zimbabwean legal practitioner before production use or customer signing.

Future app workflow:

1. Owner or admin enters party and property details.
2. Backend fills the template placeholders.
3. Tenant and landlord review the generated lease.
4. Both parties sign digitally or upload signed copies.
5. ZimHomes stores the signed document against the property, tenant, and payment records.
